Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a classic lasagna recipe that's been passed down through generations. It's a hearty, delicious dish perfect for family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 oz) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 16 oz shredded mozzarella cheese

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Drain excess fat.
    3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water to the skillet. Stir in sugar, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan cheese.
    5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer with 4 noodles, one-third of the ricotta mixture, one-third of the mozzarella cheese, and one-third of the meat sauce. Repeat layers twice more, ending with meat sauce and remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
    6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes. Let stand for 15 minutes before serving.
