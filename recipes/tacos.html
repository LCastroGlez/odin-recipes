Tacos Recipe

Description

These tacos are a quick and easy meal that's packed with flavor. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or a casual get-together with friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup diced onions

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water according to package directions.
  3. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until thickened.
  4. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  5. Spoon beef mixture into tortillas and top with cheese, tomatoes, and onions.
